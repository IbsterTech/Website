IbsterTech Commenting Policy

Welcome To IbsterTech, a tech website and community focused on Tech Tutorials.
We try our best to make sure that the discussions on our articles are the troll, spam and abuse free but we’re not omnipresent.

The comment section can be a great place for interaction with readers, writers and even for spawning stimulating conversations – it can be a great place for all of us!
To ensure that you have a good time over here at IbsterTech, please make sure to stick to the following guidelines:

  • Be polite – don’t be rude to anyone and respect others.
  • Remember there is a person behind the screen and that your words affect people. Don’t troll, aka don't be "Brain Dead" .
  • We appreciate and welcome criticism towards IbsterTech, but comments that add no value to the discussion or that are merely insulting would be deleted without exception.
  • Spam? Goodbye. This community does not tolerate spam!.. Please refrain from posting..
  • If you don’t follow any of these guidelines, not only will we remove the comment but we may also ban you from participating in future discussions.

    • If you find any comments which violate our guidelines, please make sure to let us know! – We highly appreciate your support!

    Happy commenting! 😜
