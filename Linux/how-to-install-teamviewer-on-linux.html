About TeamViewer

TeamViewer is a remote access and remote control computer software, allowing maintenance of computers and other devices.
It was first released in 2005, and its functionality has expanded step by step.
TeamViewer is proprietary software, but does not require registration and is free of charge for non-commercial use. Wikipedia

Running TeamViewer For The First Time!

After you've installed TeamViewer, a shortcut icon should be present in the application launcher of your desktop environment.
Simply search for teamviewer using its search function or look through the applications list. After clicking the icon, teamviewer should open up. Sometimes the application launcher does not update properly, and TeamViewer won't be present.
In that case logging out and back in again or restarting your computer will usually cause TeamViewer to appear.
